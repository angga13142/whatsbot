# WhatsApp Cashflow Bot - Customer Guide

**Version:** 1.0  
**For:** Customers (Self-Service)

---

## Welcome Customers!

This guide is for customers who want to check their account balance, view invoices, and make payments.

---

## Customer Commands

### Check Your Balance

```
/balance
```

Shows:

- Current outstanding balance
- Credit limit
- Available credit
- Payment history

### View Transaction History

```
/history

Or specify days:
/history 30    # Last 30 days
```

### View Invoices

```
/invoice

Or view specific invoice:
/invoice INV-202601-0001
```

Shows:

- Invoice details
- Line items
- Total amount
- Due date
- Payment status

### Payment Instructions

```
/pay [invoice-number]

Example:
/pay INV-202601-0001
```

You'll receive:

- Payment amount
- Bank account details
- Payment reference number
- Payment deadline

---

## How to Pay

1. **Get Payment Instructions:**

   ```
   /pay INV-202601-0001
   ```

2. **Transfer to Bank Account:**
   - Bank: BCA
   - Account: 1234567890
   - Name: Your Company
   - Amount: (as shown in invoice)

3. **Send Payment Confirmation:**
   Reply with:
   - Transfer receipt (photo)
   - Transfer date
   - Transfer amount

4. **Wait for Confirmation:**
   You'll receive confirmation within 1-2 business days.
