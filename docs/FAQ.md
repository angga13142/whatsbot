### Q: What is WhatsApp Cashflow Bot?

**A:** It's a financial management system accessible through WhatsApp for tracking income, expenses, debts, and generating reports.

### Q: Do I need to install anything?

**A:** No! Just WhatsApp on your phone. The bot works through WhatsApp messages.

### Q: Is my data secure?

**A:** Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely. Only authorized users can access your financial data.

### Q: Can I use it on multiple devices?

**A:** Yes, as long as you use the same WhatsApp account registered with the bot.

## üí∞ Transaction Questions

### Q: What's the minimum transaction amount?

**A:** Rp 100 minimum for all transactions.

### Q: Can I edit a transaction after saving?

**A:** Pending transactions can be edited. Approved transactions require admin approval to modify.

### Q: What if I made a mistake?

**A:** Send `/history` to find the transaction, then contact admin with the transaction ID.

### Q: Why is my transaction pending?

**A:** Transactions over Rp 1,000,000 require admin approval for security.

### Q: How long does approval take?

**A:** Usually within 24 hours during business days.

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Q: Bot not responding?

**A:**

1. Check internet connection
2. Wait 30 seconds
3. Restart WhatsApp
4. Contact admin if still not working

### Q: Error message appears?

**A:** Take screenshot and send to admin with:

- What you were trying to do
- Exact command used
- Time it occurred

### Q: Lost transaction confirmation?

**A:** Send `/history` to find it by date and amount.
